Privacy Policy

Your Privacy

Your privacy matters to us. The North Creek Estate exists to create thoughtful experiences — online and on the estate. That includes treating your personal information with the same care we give our guests. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with The North Creek, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Billing and payment information
  • Reservation details
  • Membership information
  • Event registrations
  • Tour and appointment requests
  • Information you voluntarily provide through forms or correspondence

We also collect limited technical information such as browser type, device information, and website usage analytics to improve our website and guest experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Confirm reservations and appointments
  • Process memberships and purchases
  • Communicate about events or bookings
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Improve our services and website
  • Send occasional estate news and invitations when you’ve chosen to receive them

We do not sell your personal information.

Payment Processing

Payments are processed through trusted third-party providers using industry-standard security practices. The North Creek Estate does not store complete payment card information on its own servers.

Marketing Communications

If you subscribe to estate updates, you may occasionally receive information about upcoming events, memberships, seasonal invitations, or estate news. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link included in our emails.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site and improve the experience over time. You may adjust cookie settings through your browser.

Protecting Your Information

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to safeguard the information entrusted to us. While no online system is completely secure, we continually work to protect guest information responsibly.

Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to third-party services, ticketing platforms, payment providers, social media platforms, or partner organizations. Those services maintain their own privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

The North Creek website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as our services evolve. The most current version will always appear on this page.

Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to: The North Creek Estate · events@thenorthcreek.com · 8770 North Creek Blvd., Southaven, Mississippi.
